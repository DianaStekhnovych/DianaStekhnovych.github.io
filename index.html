About the shelter
“Cozy House”
This shelter is located in a cute cozy place, where our small friends can feel most comfortable and secure. We use high-quality products for taking care for animals and also we have some specialists who work with animals, develop their skills etc. All the creatures will have the best conditions to live and love their life. Also all the animals will be examined by a vet doctor and we will try to provide a full medical service to keep creatures healthy and happy.
